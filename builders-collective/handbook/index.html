01 · WHAT IS THE CRM

YOUR BUSINESS
IN ONE screen.

Think of the CRM as the central nervous system of Builders Collective. Every lead, every call, every text — one place.

In one line
A CRM is how you keep track of everyone who's ever shown interest — from inquiry to handover.

You're set up on CloseBetter CRM — that's your branded version. Behind the scenes it's enterprise-grade software (the same kind big agencies use), but the interface is clean and made for you.

WHAT IT replaces.

Before · 01

Spreadsheets & sticky notes

"Did Hasib follow up with that REA lead?" Phone calls to find out who's doing what. Leads going cold.

After · 02

One screen, one truth

Every lead, who owns it, where it is, what's been said, what's next. From your phone or laptop.

03 · YOUR DAY-TO-DAY

WHAT YOU'LL
actually DO.

Most of the work is automated. Your job is straightforward.

01 · MORNING

Check the app

Open CloseBetter on your phone. See new leads, today's calls, follow-ups due. Two minutes, max.

02 · CALLS

Take your calls

Leads book themselves into your calendar. You just show up. The CRM has pre-sent the context.

06 · WHEN YOU TAKE A CALL

THE 4-STEP
call PROCESS.

Same simple process every time. Don't overthink it.

STEP · 01
Before · 2 minutes

Open the contact card in CloseBetter. Read the notes (where they came from, form fills, prior messages). Be ready.

Prep
STEP · 02
During · 15 minutes

Listen first. Ask about block, budget, timeline, finance. Mention the vetting angle. Take quick notes in "Notes From Call".

The call
STEP · 03
After · 5 minutes

Tag them: Audience Type + Timeline. Move the card. Trigger the post-call recap (fill 3 bullets, system sends).

Logging
STEP · 04
Within 24 hours

If qualified, send the builder shortlist. If not yet ready, the system will check back in for you. Done.

07 · 5 RULES TO LIVE BY

STICK TO THESE
and IT WORKS.

Most CRMs fail because the team stops using them. Five rules to keep that from happening.

RULE · 01
Always update the stage

After every meaningful interaction, drag the deal to the right stage. If you don't, the wrong emails go out and the dashboard lies.

RULE · 02
Tag every lead within 24h

Source is automatic. You add: Audience Type + Timeline. Takes 5 seconds, makes segmentation work.

RULE · 03
Reply to replies fast

When a lead replies, the system pauses automation and pings you. You've got 60 minutes. Don't let it sit.

RULE · 04
Never delete a contact

If they're not buying, move them to "Not Yet Ready" or "Lost". Don't delete. We track everything for future re-engagement.

RULE · 05
Check the dashboard weekly

Every Monday, look at where leads are stalling and where spend is converting. Adjust. ↗ Open dashboard

08 · GLOSSARY

THE WORDS
YOU'LL hear.

CRM
Customer Relationship Management — the software that tracks every lead, every conversation, every deal. Yours is CloseBetter.
Pipeline
The visual board showing every deal and what stage it's in. Drag cards across as deals progress.
Lead source
Where a lead came from (REA / Meta / Google / Website / Phone). Auto-tagged by the system.
Discovery call
The 15-minute call you take to qualify a new lead. Round-robin distributed across the 4 of you.
PPA
Preliminary Property Agreement — signed with a builder before the formal HIA contract. Small deposit ($1–3K). Your commission locks in here.
Building Contract
The formal HIA contract. Signed after PPA + plans finalised. Triggers the inspector workflow.
Workflow
An automated sequence of emails, SMS and tasks triggered by a specific event.
Nurture
Multi-day sequence for leads who haven't booked yet. 21 days, then long-term follow-up.
Round-robin
How leads distribute across the team — auto-rotated so everyone gets an equal share.
VSL
Video Sales Letter — the 2-3 minute explainer on the landing page. Pre-sells before the call.
Inspector
The independent third-party building inspector who does random site visits during builds. Fee built into the package.
AI voice agent
Answers calls 8pm–6am. Takes details, books calls. Doesn't replace you, just makes sure no lead is lost overnight.
