01 · Positioning

we're a broker, not a builder.

What we're not
not a builder.
We don't pour slabs, frame walls, or hand over keys. The brand should never speak in trade language ("handover folder", "PC sum", "lockup") as if the work is ours.
What we are
the building broker.
We sit on the buyer's side. We shortlist builders, secure land, line up finance. The buyer pays nothing extra. We're paid a fixed fee from the builder side, like an in-house consultant who happens to work for the customer.
Who we're for
perth buyers ready to build.
First home buyers, upgraders, downsizers, turnkey buyers. Anyone who'd otherwise walk into one display village and trust whoever's standing there. We give them the shortlist.
every brand decision flows from this. if it reads like a builder talking, it's wrong. if it reads like a mortgage broker who handles the whole build, it's right.

06 · Voice & Tone

confident. specific. on your side.

"you wouldn't buy a mortgage without a broker.
why would you buy a home you have to build?"
First Appointment · The Pitch
Most first home buyers walk into one display village, meet one consultant, sign the contract that consultant is paid to sell. We line up four builders for the same brief, same budget, same suburb. You see the prices side by side. You pick the one that fits. Our fee comes out of the builder's pocket, not yours.

Yes

  • Real numbers: FHOG $10K, LOE in 48 hours, 4 builders compared, $30K typical wholesale saving.
  • Buyer-side language: "your shortlist", "your side of the table", "we work for you".
  • Italic serif for the promise. Mono for the numbers. Sans for the explainer.
  • Name the suburb, the price band, the buyer type (first home / upgrader / downsizer).

No

  • Builder cosplay: "passionate craftsmanship", "your dream home", "every fixture matters".
  • "Where Your Home Journey Finds it Match" (typo + Mad-Libs structure, your current cover).
  • Em-dashes, semicolons, exclamation marks, "let's get you into your dream home!"
  • Stock photo metaphors: bear traps, dancing families, confused men holding phones.

08 · Tagline Directions

pick one and ride it.

Recommended · The Wholesale Frame
go direct, pay retail.
come through us, pay wholesale.
Counterintuitive (most buyers assume direct = cheapest). Parallel structure makes it sticky. Earns the savings stamp, the shortlist card, the dollar mono pattern. Differentiates from every "we make building easy" broker tagline.
The Mortgage-Broker Analogy
you wouldn't buy a mortgage without a broker.
why buy a home you have to build?
Anchors to a category buyers already understand. Good for explainer content and YouTube ad scripts.
The Side-of-Table Frame
your side of the table.
perth's building broker.
Plainest version. Good as the email signature line and the website fold-line.
