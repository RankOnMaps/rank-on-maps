03 · WHY WE EXIST
CHAPTER · 02

The problem
we solve.

Most people who build in Perth get burned by one of four things. We exist to make sure none of them happen to you.

PROBLEM · 01
Builders going under mid-build

Household-name WA builders have collapsed in the last two years. Deposits gone, builds half-finished, families stuck paying rent and a mortgage. We vet builder financial health before they go on your shortlist.

PROBLEM · 02
Quotes that start at $299K and end at $420K

Site costs. Fill. Retaining walls. "Promotional inclusions" that aren't actually included. We decode every quote so you see the real price before you sign.

PROBLEM · 03
Quotes designed to be uncomparable

No two builder quotes are structured the same way — by design. We line them up apples-to-apples so you can actually choose.

PROBLEM · 04
No one watching the build

Most buyers find out about issues after handover. We send a third-party building inspector to your site during construction. Random visits. Issues caught early.

08 · BUILD JOURNEY
CHAPTER · 07

From PPA
to handover.

Here's exactly what happens between signing the preliminary agreement and getting your keys.

  1. 01 · PPAPreliminary Property Agreement signed. Small deposit ($1-3K) locks in your price. Builder draws up plans.
  2. 02 · PLANSPlans finalised + specs locked. Typically 4-8 weeks. You sign off on everything.
  3. 03 · FINANCEFormal finance approval. Confirmed with your lender. Pre-approval converted to actual.
  4. 04 · CONTRACTFull HIA building contract signed. We audit it before you sign. Inspector is engaged.
  5. 05 · SLABSite works + slab pour. First independent inspection. Report sent to you.
  6. 06 · FRAMEFrame up. Second inspection. Catches structural issues early.
  7. 07 · LOCKUPRoof, windows, doors in. Third inspection. Weatherproofing checked.
  8. 08 · FIXINGInternal fit-off. Fourth inspection. Finishes checked.
  9. 09 · HANDOVERKeys delivered. Final inspection. Defects list. You move in.
