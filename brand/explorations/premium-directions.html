Which one?

This isn't a final commitment · pick a direction, or mix two ("A's color with C's type"), and I'll apply it across the existing kit and templates. You can also point at a specific cell ("the deck slide in B is right but the caption frame is too cold") and we'll adjust.

A · EDITORIAL OPERATOR

Pick this if you want the brand to feel written. Best fit for: long-form YouTube, sales decks, podcast intros. Risk: italic serif moments need protection · easy for editors to over-use.

B · ARCHITECT

Pick this if you want the brand to feel institutional. Best fit for: pitch decks, the website, anything where seriousness sells. Risk: harder to translate into kinetic short-form without the system softening.

C · SOFT-TECH

Pick this if you want the brand to feel calmly expensive. Best fit for: most things · most flexible across surfaces. Risk: closest to your current direction, so the upgrade is subtle (which can be a feature, not a bug).
