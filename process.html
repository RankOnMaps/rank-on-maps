The 90-day playbook

Everything we do.
In the order we do it.

Week 1 · Free

1. Audit

Before we touch a thing, we spend 4–6 hours digging into your business, your competitors, and your map pack. You get a deep-dive report that's yours to keep, even if you decide not to work with us.

  • Full GMB health check (categories, attributes, NAP, photos)
  • Top 5 competitor breakdown across your service area
  • Live geogrid scan showing your current rankings
  • Citation, schema and on-page audit
  • Honest, no-fluff recommendations
1
2
Week 2 · Onboarding

2. Strategy

We sit down with you for a 90-minute strategy call. Together, we lock in the suburbs you want to win, the services you want to push, and the ranking targets we'll commit to.

  • 90-minute deep-dive strategy call
  • Suburb & keyword priority list
  • 90-day ranking forecast and milestones
  • Communication and reporting cadence agreed
  • Access & credential handover (we handle the boring stuff)
Weeks 3–6 · Foundation

3. Optimize

This is where the foundation gets rebuilt. We rip apart your GMB, fix every citation, rewrite your on-page, and bake in the schema that Google needs to take you seriously.

  • GMB rebuild: categories, services, attributes, descriptions
  • 200+ citation audit, clean-up and rebuild
  • On-page rewrites for every city + service combination
  • Local schema markup added across the site
  • Site speed and Core Web Vitals fixes
3
4
Weeks 7–12 · Compound

4. Amplify

The foundation is set. Now we feed the machine. Every week we publish posts, photos, reviews and links, all the signals Google needs to push you up the rankings, month after month.

  • Weekly Google Posts and photo uploads
  • Review generation funnel turned on
  • White-hat local link building campaigns
  • Content production for service area pages
  • Spam fighting and competitor reporting
Ongoing · Forever

5. Report

Every Monday morning you get a digest email with where you ranked last week, what we did, and what's coming next. Once a month, we jump on a call to walk through the geogrid together.

  • Live geogrid dashboard updated weekly
  • Monday morning email digest
  • Monthly 30-minute strategy review call
  • Real-time alerts when rankings shift
  • Quarterly business review with leadership
